# Throned Business Thesis

Throned monetizes by strengthening meaningful competition, not interrupting attention.

## Core Principle

The future of advertising is participatory:
- not "look at this"
- but "be part of this"

Brands should fund and elevate competition ecosystems where contenders earn status through performance.

## Monetization Doctrine

- Competition integrity is non-negotiable.
- Sponsored systems must not distort ranking legitimacy.
- Brand presence should feel like patronage of meaningful competition.
- Monetization should increase aspiration, participation, and achievement memory.

## Acceptable Commercial Formats

- Sponsored arena seasons
- Regional qualifiers and ladders
- Creator or community championships
- Challenge prizes tied to transparent criteria
- Advancement opportunities (exposure, real-world rewards, pathways)

## Unacceptable Formats

- Interruptive ad slots that break battle immersion
- Outcome influence tied to spend
- Pay-to-win ranking movement
- Brand-first overlays that dominate battle flow
- Generic attention extraction patterns

## Success Criteria

Commercial programs should increase:
- emotional investment in contenders
- participation in meaningful competition
- rivalry and narrative continuity
- trust in hierarchy legitimacy

If revenue grows while emotional legitimacy declines, the system is off doctrine.
