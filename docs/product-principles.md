# Product Principles

Users should feel like they are inside a live contest,
not operating a UI.

The app should feel:
- modern
- slick
- kinetic
- immersive
- native
- minimal

The videos should dominate the screen.

UI chrome should be minimized aggressively.

Buttons are a last resort.

Primary interactions should be gesture-based.

Core gestures:
- swipe outward = throw away loser
- swipe inward = switch arena
- tap = pause/resume
- hold = reveal details
- drag = leaderboard

The experience should feel emotionally competitive.

Users are not selecting favorites.
They are eliminating losers.

The VS seam is sacred.
It represents direct competition.

Arena switching should feel fluid and fast.

The app should reward momentum and exploration.

Nothing should feel corporate or dashboard-like.

The app should feel alive.
