# Throwned Vision

Throwned is a competitive ranking platform for subjective content.

Users compete head-to-head in live content battles.

The core mechanic is NOT liking content.
The mechanic is THROWING AWAY the loser.

The experience should feel visceral, immediate, social, and addictive.

Some get thrown.
Some become known.
The best are Throwned.

The platform exists to answer:

"Where do I fit in the world?"

Instead of passive feeds and vanity metrics,
Throwned creates direct competition between content.

The app should feel like:
- a live contest
- an arena
- a constant climb or fall in status

The best content rises because it defeats other content directly.

This is not just for entertainment.

The ranking engine could eventually apply to:
- recruiting
- sports
- music
- comedy
- marketing
- fan engagement
- brand competitions
- decision-making
- subjective evaluation systems

The long-term vision is a universal competitive ranking engine for subjective media.
