# Throned Vision

Throned is a competitive media system and emotional hierarchy engine for subjective arenas.

Users do not browse a feed. They enter a live contest.

Core mechanic:
- two contenders compete
- the user throws away the loser
- the winner survives
- hierarchy evolves through direct comparison

The product should feel:
- consequential
- immersive
- cinematic
- tribal
- aspirational

Status is earned, defended, and lost in public.

## Long-Horizon Direction

Throned becomes a meaningful competition ecosystem where:
- contenders build identity through performance
- users form participatory fandom around ascent and rivalry
- arenas generate cultural narratives, not passive consumption loops

## Expansion Surface

The competitive model can extend to domains where subjective judgment matters, including:
- music
- comedy
- storytelling
- design
- sport-adjacent skills
- culture and style

The platform stays doctrine-aligned only if hierarchy legitimacy and emotional investment remain central.
